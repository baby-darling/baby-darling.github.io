HIS STORY

Cheburashka is a funny little animal, unknown to science, who lives in the tropical forest. He accidentally gets into a crate of oranges, eats his fill, and falls asleep. Cheburashka is a name invented by the puzzled director of the shop where he is found. The salesman sits him on the table, but his paws are numb after the long time spent in the crate, and he tumbles down.

HIS FRIENDS

Cheburashka has many friends of all kinds. They gathered every evening to chat and take coffee. They set up a Friendship House - a kind of friend's exchange, to help everyone looking for friends. His best friend used to be Crocodile Gena back in his motherland but currently his best friend is named Anna. She is also his mamyte. She treats him very well and her friend Vid is also sometimes nice.

HIS SKILLS

Cheburashka is a master of many different skills. He really likes twerking but sometimes it's inappropriate, so in those times he either sings or does acrobatics. He is a big fan of lounging - that is his best ability. But when he's not feeling lazy then he loves to cuddle and snuggle. His favourite activity to cool down is to eat all kinds of sweets or oranges.
